HINTERLAND · A PROCEDURAL ATLAS OF WHO PAYS

A generator of unequal worlds

Every map this makes is an argument about inequality: who gets served and who sits in darkness, who breathes the poison and who ships it downstream, who holds the gates and who pays the toll — none of it painted, all of it emergent from blind geology, a cost-gated grid, and factions that seize, tax, and go to war. The fantasy is the carrier; the attribute table is the argument. Seeded, reproducible, QGIS-ready.
